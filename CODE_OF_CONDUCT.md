# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and constructive experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Being respectful and kind in all interactions
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community
- Crediting others' contributions appropriately

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Disruptive or trolling behavior in issues, PRs, or discussions

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other project-related communication.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to **bhavikunaal@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially. Maintainers reserve the right to remove contributions or ban participants who violate these standards.
